What is WindowJackie?


The Artist

WindowJackie was started in 2010 by Jackie Harris. Originally a Miami, Florida artist relocated to Orlando, Florida where she is actively painting and creating as often as possible. No project is too big, nor too small. She is always looking for the next challenge and is ready to take on your commission. In addition to painting, Jackie often collaborates with Alexander Kalik (formerly Warehouse Director of Scotty Wonders) on woodworking, metalwork, and handmade furniture.

The Art

Artwork is all original ideas and creations by WindowJackie. Each piece is uniquely painted and created to satisfy each client. No piece will ever be sold if it does not meet WindowJackie standards. Mediums used include acrylic paint, chalk pastel, colored pencil, Conte Crayon, charcoal, graphite, woodworks, fabric, paper, and much more to fit the needs of the project. WindowJackie paintings can be found all across the country - from California to Pennsylvania to Alabama, etc. If you are not satisfied by the final product, it can and will be edited to meet your desire. To order or to discuss any commissions, please email windowjackie@gmail.com. Our goal at WindowJackie is to make sure each client recieves a product that is not only good quality, but meets and exceeds your satisfaction.
